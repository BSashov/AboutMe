Igniting
the Spark

From an early age, I was fascinated by the beauty of mathematics and the boundless potential of technology. It wasn't until I discovered the world of design, however, that I truly found my calling. I immersed myself in every aspect of this thrilling field, studying at the prestigious High School of Mathematics and the New Bulgarian University's cutting-edge Computer Graphics and Design program.

Journey in the World of Design

As my skills grew, I was eager to test myself in the fast-paced world of design. I began my journey as a lecturer, sharing my love of technology and design with others. From there, I took on challenging roles in pre-press and design at a top printing house before making the bold move to Germany. There, I left my mark on the design world by creating powerful brand identities and developing a modern job searching platform for medical experts.

Learn, Aspire,
Achieve

But my passion for design didn't stop there. I continued to push the boundaries of what was possible, taking on a role at a leading marketing agency. There, I spearheaded the creation and marketing of a comprehensive website builder that revolutionized the way pharmacies and small pharmaceutical companies showcased their services and products. The stunning designs I created not only caught the eye of customers but earned critical acclaim from industry experts as well.

Designing
the Future

As I look towards the future, I'm excited to see where my passion for design will take me next. With every project, I'm eager to take on new challenges and continue honing my skills. I'm confident that my unique background in mathematics, technology, and design, combined with my boundless creativity, make me an asset to any team. So why not join me on this thrilling journey and let's create something amazing together?
